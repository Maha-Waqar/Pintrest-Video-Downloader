{% trans "How to File a DMCA Notice (Takedown Request)" %}

{% trans "If you own a copyrighted video or content and you believe it is accessible via PinCatch in violation of your rights, send us a written notice with:" %}

  • {% trans "Your full name, address, phone number, and email address" %}
  • {% trans "A description (title, URL, etc.) of your copyrighted work" %}
  • {% trans "The exact URL on PinCatch (or in our logs) where the content appears" %}
  • {% trans "A statement that you believe in good faith that the use is not allowed by the copyright owner" %}
  • {% trans "A statement under penalty of perjury that the information is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized agent" %}
  • {% trans "Your electronic signature (or physical signature)" %}

{% trans "Send your notice via email to: dmca@pincatch.com" %}

{% trans "Once we get a valid DMCA notice, we will:" %}
  • {% trans "Remove or disable access to the infringing content" %}
  • {% trans "Notify the user who requested the content" %}
  • {% trans "Keep records of the request" %}
{% trans " Counter-Notice by the User" %}

  • {% trans "Description of content removed and its previous location" %}
  • {% trans "Statement that under penalty of perjury, they believe the content was wrongly removed" %}

  • {% trans "Their signature" %}

{% trans "If valid, we may restore the content after waiting the required time (e.g., 10-14 business days) unless the copyright owner files a lawsuit." %}
