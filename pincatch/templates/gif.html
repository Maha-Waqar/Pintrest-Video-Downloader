Understanding GIF Quality: 1080p vs 2K vs 4K

Video quality depends on resolution, which determines the sharpness of the image:

  • 1080p (Full HD): Perfect for phones and tablets, offering excellent quality without large file sizes. Ideal for everyday use.
  • 2K: Provides slightly better quality than 1080p, suitable for larger screens or content you plan to edit.
  • 4K (Ultra HD): Offers the highest level of detail, with four times the pixels of 1080p. Best for travel videos or design content where clarity is essential. Keep in mind that 4K files are larger but deliver unmatched quality.
